Travel

Pictures from my travels

Thailand

I have a large interest in travel and have been to many different countries
However the country that was by far the most interesting was Thailand.
My family and I travelled there in 2016 and visited 3 different areas; Bangkok, Koh Samui and Changmai.
The best ecperience from it all was when we visted an elephant sanctuary in Changmai.
We got to feed the elephants and then bathe them in a river. It was breathtaking.
The food over there was unbelievable aswell, nothing like what we have at home.

New York

In Transition year in school, our year undertook the annual trip to the Big Apple, And what a trip it was.
The highlight of the trip in my eyes was when we visited Maddison Square Garden.
We watched two NBA games and I was awestruck by the pace and excitement of the whole thing
The food over there was glorious; pancakes, burgers and pizza galore, stereotypically American.
The view atop the Empire State Building is stupendous but impossible to describe as you've got to be there.
